My Dog Phoebe

My dog Phoebe joined our family on January 9th 2013. I went with my father and brother to pick her up from a rescue shelter. As soon as we arrived she came right up to us to play. As we were playing with her and the other dogs, the caretaker yelled at other dogs, and Phoebe went to hide. We all knew then that we had to have her to give her a good life.

Training Begins

We started training Phoebe almost as soon as we got her. We started by taking her out to the front yard with us when we went out. If while we were out she would try to leave the yard without our ok, then we would yell to get her attention, and then take her inside. She learned to stay within the yard until we said so within the first month.

Neighborhood Mascot

Through the years during our walks we managed to get Phoebe trained to the point that she no longer needs a leash. She will obey all commands we give her immediately. All through the neighborhood everyone knows Phoebe as part of our family. I had a neighbor that I don't really know well ask me at my job how Phoebe was doing. She goes with us to all of the neighborhood watch meetings, and thanks to that she became well known to all of the neighbors as the mascot.
